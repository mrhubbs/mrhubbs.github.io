Philosophy

Human-centric design.

Making Software for People

I'm passionate about making technology that's truly designed for humans–technology that takes into account how we naturally think, learn, and interact with the world. Such software should be:

 

Intuitive

Make use of natural symbols, colors,
and thought processes.

 

Clear

Communicate at a glance what an app is doing and what actions are possible.

 

Beautiful

Important for its own sake, beauty also
leads to good design.
