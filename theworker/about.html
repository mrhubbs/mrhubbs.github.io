Tech Bio

As a child, a strong interest in design led to hours reading about Tom Swift, Jr.'s inventions and building with Legos. Lego Mindstorms introduced me to programming. Shortly thereafter I began learning C on Linux.

Ten years later, I am fluent in C and Python, familiar with a half-dozen other languages, and well-versed in web technologies. I'm equally comfortable writing optimized C-code, cross-platform desktop applications, websites, and Android apps (using Kivy, my Java is a little rusty).

I really enjoy my work. Over-and-above the "technical thrill," though, I am extremely passionate about creating simple, practical, elegant and beautiful programs that truly work for people.
