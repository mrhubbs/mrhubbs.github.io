Philosophy

Making Software for People

Technology ought to be made for humans–designers must consider how we naturally think, learn, and interact with the world.

Human-centric software should be:

 

Intuitive

Make use of natural symbols, colors, and thought processes.

 

Clear

Communicate at a glance what an app is doing and what actions are possible.

 

Beautiful

Important for its own sake, beauty also leads to good design.
